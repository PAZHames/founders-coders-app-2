My name is Phoebe. I have loved the last 8 years working as an actor and teacher, but when the pandemic hit so did reality and I made the ultimately very freeing decision to move away from an industry that was no longer serving me. I have always been a keen problem solver and although I loved acting, it lacks the ability to consistently work on and fix problems.

I realised I needed more tangible gains and momentum in my life. In my free time, I go to a lot of exhibitions, both art and museum and during my Masters, I took a module in ‘Digitising Cultural Heritage’, which was one of the impetuses for learning to code. I found the discussions around open source data and who owns digital content fascinating and wanted to contribute practically to this discussion, beyond my education in Classics.

As such, on a bit of a whim, I picked up a coding intro with SheCodes about a year ago. And I was hooked. I have not found flow like that in any other part of my life for years and it is addictive. I now finish each day with my brain aching but I am so, so happy.
